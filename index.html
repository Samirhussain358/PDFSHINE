Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDFShine completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No subscriptions, no daily limits, no premium plans. Every tool is free forever.
Are my files safe? Do you store them?
Your files are 100% safe. PDFShine runs entirely in your browser — no file is ever sent to our servers. Your documents never leave your device.
Do I need to create an account?
No account, no email, no signup. Just open any tool and use it immediately.
Does PDFShine work on mobile phones?
Yes! Fully responsive — works on Android, iPhone, tablets and desktops. No app needed.
Is there a file size limit?
Processing happens in your browser, so the limit depends on your device memory. Most PDFs under 100MB work perfectly.
