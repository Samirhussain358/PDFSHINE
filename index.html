Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDFShine completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No subscriptions, no daily limits, no premium plans. Every tool is free forever.
Are my files safe? Do you store them?
Your files are 100% safe. PDFShine runs entirely in your browser — no file is ever sent to our servers. Your documents never leave your device.
Do I need to create an account?
No account, no email, no signup needed. Just open any tool and start using it immediately.
Does PDFShine work on mobile phones?
Yes! PDFShine is fully responsive and works on Android, iPhone, tablets and desktops. No app download needed.
Is there a file size limit?
Since all processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device memory. Most PDFs under 100MB work perfectly.
How is PDFShine different from iLovePDF or Smallpdf?
Unlike iLovePDF and Smallpdf which upload files to their servers and limit free usage, PDFShine processes everything locally — zero uploads, zero limits, zero watermarks, completely free.

Free PDF Tools That Work In Your Browser — No Upload Required

PDFShine is a free online platform offering 28+ PDF and document tools that run entirely in your browser. Whether you need to merge PDF files, split a PDF, compress PDF, protect a PDF with a password, unlock a PDF, or convert images to PDF — PDFShine handles it all instantly without uploading your files anywhere.

New tools include Protect PDF (add 128-bit password encryption), Unlock PDF (remove passwords), Remove Pages, Organize PDF (drag to reorder pages with visual thumbnails), and a full PDF Viewer — all completely free.

Better Than iLovePDF and Smallpdf — No Limits, No Uploads

Unlike iLovePDF and Smallpdf which send your files to remote servers, impose daily limits on free users, and sometimes add watermarks — PDFShine uses open-source libraries (PDF-lib, PDF.js, Tesseract.js) to process everything locally. This means complete privacy, no file size server quotas, and instant results.
