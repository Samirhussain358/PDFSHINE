Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDFShine completely free?
Yes — 100% free. No subscriptions, no daily limits, no premium plans. Every single tool is free forever.
Are my files safe? Do you store them?
Your files are 100% safe. PDFShine runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No file is ever sent to our servers. Your documents never leave your device.
Do I need to create an account?
No account, no email, no signup required. Just open any tool and start using it immediately.
Does PDFShine work on mobile phones?
Yes! PDFShine is fully responsive and works on Android, iPhone, tablets and desktop computers. No app download needed.
Is there a file size limit?
Since all processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's available memory. Most PDFs under 100MB work perfectly. For very large files, use a desktop browser.
