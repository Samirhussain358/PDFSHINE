Why Use DocZap for Your PDF & Document Needs?

DocZap is a free online PDF and document tools platform that works entirely in your browser — no file ever leaves your device. Whether you need to merge PDF files, split a PDF into pages, compress a large PDF, or convert images to PDF, DocZap does it instantly without requiring a signup or account.

Beyond PDF tools, DocZap also includes a powerful word counter and character counter, a text case converter (UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, camelCase, snake_case), an image to text OCR tool, a lorem ipsum generator, and utilities like age calculator, percentage calculator, password generator, QR code generator, and Base64 encoder/decoder.

Unlike other PDF tools that upload your files to their servers, DocZap processes everything locally using PDF-lib, PDF.js, and Tesseract.js. Your documents stay private. Your data stays yours. 100% free, forever.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DocZap completely free to use?

Yes. DocZap is 100% free. All tools work without any signup, subscription, or payment. No hidden charges.

Are my files safe when using DocZap?

Absolutely. All files are processed in your browser using JavaScript. No file is ever uploaded to any server — making DocZap the most private way to work with PDFs online.

Do I need to create an account?

No signup or account required. Just open DocZap in any browser and start using any tool instantly. No email, no password.

What PDF tools does DocZap offer?

DocZap offers: Merge PDF, Split PDF, Compress PDF, Rotate PDF, Watermark PDF, PDF Info Viewer, Image to PDF, and PDF to Image conversion — all free and browser-based.

Does DocZap work on mobile phones?

Yes. DocZap is fully responsive and works on Android, iPhone, tablets, and desktops. No app download required.

Is there a file size limit?

Since processing is in-browser, the limit depends on your device memory. Most PDFs under 100MB work perfectly. For very large files, use a desktop browser with enough RAM.

How is DocZap different from ilovepdf or smallpdf?

Unlike ilovepdf and smallpdf which upload files to their servers, DocZap processes everything locally. Zero privacy risk, no upload time, no server quotas, works even offline once loaded — and completely free with no daily limits.
