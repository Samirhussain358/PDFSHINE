Why Use PDFShine for Your PDF & Document Needs?

PDFShine is a free online PDF and document tools platform that works entirely in your browser — no file ever leaves your device. Whether you need to merge PDF files, split a PDF into pages, compress a large PDF, or convert images to PDF, PDFShine does it instantly without requiring a signup or account.

Beyond PDF tools, PDFShine also includes a powerful word counter and character counter, a text case converter (UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, camelCase, snake_case), an image to text OCR tool, a lorem ipsum generator, and utilities like age calculator, percentage calculator, password generator, QR code generator, and Base64 encoder/decoder.

Unlike other PDF tools that upload your files to their servers, PDFShine processes everything locally using PDF-lib, PDF.js, and Tesseract.js. Your documents stay private. Your data stays yours. 100% free, forever.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDFShine completely free to use?

Yes. PDFShine is 100% free. All tools work without any signup, subscription, or payment. No hidden charges.

Are my files safe when using PDFShine?

Absolutely. All files are processed in your browser using JavaScript. No file is ever uploaded to any server — making PDFShine the most private way to work with PDFs online.

Do I need to create an account?

No signup or account required. Just open PDFShine in any browser and start using any tool instantly. No email, no password.

What PDF tools does PDFShine offer?

PDFShine offers: Merge PDF, Split PDF, Compress PDF, Rotate PDF, Watermark PDF, PDF Info Viewer, Image to PDF, and PDF to Image conversion — all free and browser-based.

Does PDFShine work on mobile phones?

Yes. PDFShine is fully responsive and works on Android, iPhone, tablets, and desktops. No app download required.

Is there a file size limit?

Since processing is in-browser, the limit depends on your device memory. Most PDFs under 100MB work perfectly. For very large files, use a desktop browser with enough RAM.

How is PDFShine different from ilovepdf or smallpdf?

Unlike ilovepdf and smallpdf which upload files to their servers, PDFShine processes everything locally. Zero privacy risk, no upload time, no server quotas, works even offline once loaded — and completely free with no daily limits.
